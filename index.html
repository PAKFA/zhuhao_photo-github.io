About Me

I am now an Associate Researcher in the Computational Imaging Technology & Engineering Lab in Nanjing Univeristy. My research focuses on Computational Imaging, I'm currently focused on light field imaging, including spatial/angular super-resolution and image based rendering.

I'm currently working with Prof. Xun Cao. For students who are interested and have passion in our research area, welcome to join us!
